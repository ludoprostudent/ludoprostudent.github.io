Our services

Individual Service

This is a service that is suited for a dog that doesn't need a full package yet. It includes only one of the following services, of your choosing:

  • Ear cleaning
  • Nail trimming
  • Bathing
Prices
  • Small (2-25lbs): $30
  • Medium (25-75lbs): $40
  • Large (75+lbs): $50
Regular Grooming Package

This is a service ideal for any dogs looking to have an all over haircut with all the trimmings! This includes a deep cleaning and hydrating bath, blow dry, an all-over fur trim/cut including trimming of the face, sanitary trim, shaving of the fur between the pads and around the feet, a nail trimming, and ear cleaning!

Prices
  • Small (2-25lbs): $100
  • Medium (25-75lbs): $150
  • Large (75+lbs): $200
Premium Grooming Package

This service is perfect for spoiled fur babies. On top of everything included in the regular package, the premium package provides you with the following accessories to bring home to keep caring for your dog:

  • 1 Deodorizer (from a selection in house)
  • 1 Bag of dog treats (vegan option available)
  • 1 Shampoo bottle customized to your dog's fur
  • 1 Dog bandana (Multiple colors available)
Prices
  • Small (2-25lbs): $200
  • Medium (25-75lbs): $250
  • Large (75+lbs): $300
